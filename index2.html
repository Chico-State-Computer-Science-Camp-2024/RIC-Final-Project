Sometime during the 60th election, aliens from the planet Pinpon invaded our
country. After violently disintegrating both candidates live on Kimmy Jimmel’s late
night show, they decided that any American’s social status will be determined by
their skill in the game of table tennis and it will be the only legal way of winning an
argument. Naturally, people don’t care and keep performing so called “tik tok
dances” or immersing themselves in braincell-deleting content.
The next day, in the deadly heat of Chico California, two table tennis tryhards Pizi and
Jigob plan to overthrow their summer camp using these new laws.
It’s now time for you to accompany these two on their journey where only the winner
will stay on.
